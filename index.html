Welcome to the BattleShip Game

The rules are as follows: Aircraft Carrier takes up 5 spaces

BattleShip takes up four

Destoryer takes up three

Submarine takes up two

Patrol Boat takes up one

Your job is to click a certain part on the grid, if you hit(red), you earn another chance to strike a torpedo, if you miss the spot is white and computer gets a chance.

You are a highly trained commandent. You will rearrange your ships and start combat at the start of the game.
The objective is to destroy all of your opponent's ships, and you will attempt to pinpoint their locations by making educated guesses about where they are. You will be competing against a well-versed commandent(computer) that has yet to lose. Wishing you luck, commandent, don't disappoint your country.
